For exterior surfaces, it’s best to use high-quality acrylic latex paints. They offer excellent durability, resistance to weather, and are easy to clean. Look for paints that provide UV protection and have mildew-resistant properties to ensure a long-lasting finish.

Choosing the right color depends on the mood you want to create and the lighting in the room. Lighter colors can make a space feel larger and brighter, while darker colors add warmth and coziness. Consider using color samples and testing them in different lighting conditions before making your final decision.

Before painting, ensure the walls are clean, dry, and free of cracks. Repair any damage and sand the surfaces to create a smooth base. Protect floors and furniture with drop cloths and tape off edges to ensure clean lines. Priming the walls is also recommended for better paint adhesion and coverage.

To extend the life of your paint job, ensure proper surface preparation and use high-quality paints and primers. Apply at least two coats for better durability and protection. Additionally, regular maintenance, such as cleaning and touch-ups, will help preserve the paint’s appearance over time.
